JustBook Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Introduction

JustBook respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the JustBook app.

Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, and basic profile details
  • Location data to show nearby workers or customers
  • Service preferences and booking details
  • App usage and interaction data

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect workers and customers directly
  • To improve the JustBook app experience
  • To maintain safety, trust, and transparency
  • To provide customer support and service updates

Data Sharing

JustBook does not sell or rent your personal data. Information is shared only between workers and customers for booking-related purposes.

Payments

Payments are made directly between customers and workers. JustBook does not store any sensitive payment information.

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

User Rights

  • You may edit or update your profile information
  • You may request deletion of your account
  • You control the information you choose to share

Policy Updates

JustBook may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
