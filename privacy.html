JustBook Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

  • JustBook respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.
  • This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the JustBook app.
  • We may collect your name, phone number, basic profile details, location data, service preferences, booking details, and app usage data.
  • Your information is used to connect workers and customers, improve app experience, maintain safety and transparency, and provide support and updates.
  • JustBook does not sell or rent your personal data.
  • Information is shared only between workers and customers for booking-related purposes.
  • Payments are made directly between customers and workers, and JustBook does not store sensitive payment information.
  • We use reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.
  • You may edit or update your profile information, request account deletion, and control the information you choose to share.
  • JustBook may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and changes will be reflected on this page.
  • If you have any questions, contact us at support@justbook.co.in or call 7819844425.
